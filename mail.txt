do "View Original" from the drop down menu in the upper right corner of the
email.

You should see a text file of a raw email.  The goal is to parse that and
extract relevant fields.

This is actually a very difficult thing and proper implementation could take
months.  However, it's possible to do the basics, without handling all of
the intricacies, in as little as 2 but no more than 8 hours of coding time
(research not included in this time estimate).

   non-email-specific libraries you want.

   Don't forget the requirements from #3!
